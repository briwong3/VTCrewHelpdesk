What are the ticket prices? ⌄

first-timer for 1 adult and 1 child would be $49, a license will be issued at the end of the session. second-timer onwards for the same 1 adult and 1 child would be $38. we are hourly sessioned.

Customer wants extension? ⌄

Inform customer of the extension price ($19) and check slot availability before extending.

Customer wants to add additional adult? ⌄

Inform them that each additional adult will be at $15.

Customer that is not first-timer wants a new license? ⌄

Inform them replacement of license is $10.

Cannot find customer record in registration form for non first-timer? ⌄

Check thoroughly, like email as cannot find the phone number might due to incomplete phone number, if really cant find ask them show prove (pictures of them in vt, email or past records).

How to use QuickStats? ⌄

For every 15 or 30min (when no customer), update the quickstats by keying walkin numbers for $38 Drivers Pass, voucher, bundle and AP (pls select correct type of ticket).

How to redeem bundle? ⌄

Go to the CRM, type in customer phone number and check if bundle is valid, if yes, click on 'Book' and fill up the corresponding inputs, then once successful it will auto be in the webba and tabulated in the quickstats so do not key into quickstats also (if not double count).
